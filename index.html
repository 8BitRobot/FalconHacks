What is FalconHacks? Our Mission
FalconHacks is an annual hackathon - an event for humans, not just coders to create and present an idea or solution - for high schoolers. Teams will go through workshops to hone their skills and work together to create a prototype and presentation. Get inspired by your peers, and have a blast bringing your dreams to life! To inspire our community to solve real-world problems through computer science. With the rise of computers in everyday life, programming continues to become more valuable and relevant from science to consumer technology. At FalconHacks, we aim to introduce students to computer science fundamentals and launch the next generation of strong coders.
Collaborate
Meet and collaborate with high schoolers from all over the Bay Area. Concepts grow best in a diverse and active environment.
Learn
Go through our workshops taught by talented and clever highschoolers. With a startup CRO, CTO, a RoboRAVE winner, and a USACO Finalist, our team will empower you to implement your idea.
Innovate
With an open theme and a venue offering food and entertainment, you can devote more time to coming up with and implementing your ideas.
